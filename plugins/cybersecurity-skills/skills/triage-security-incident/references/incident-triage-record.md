# Incident Triage Record

```markdown
- Incident ID/title/status:
- Reporter/detection source/time:
- Incident lead and decision owners:
- Observed facts and confidence:
- Potentially affected assets/identities/data/users:
- Time window and ongoing behavior:
- Business/service/privacy/safety impact:
- Immediate harm-reduction action and evidence tradeoff:
- Evidence sources preserved/missing:
- Communication and notification contacts:
- Open hypotheses and disconfirming evidence:
- Next response phase and exit criteria:
```

Use current [NIST SP 800-61 Rev. 3](https://csrc.nist.gov/pubs/sp/800/61/r3/final). Treat incident response as part of ongoing risk management, preparation, detection, response, and recovery rather than a one-time linear checklist.
